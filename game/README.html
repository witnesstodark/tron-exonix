QIX: Territory Reveal — Playable Prototype v1

Godot 4.7GDScriptXonix / AirXonix mechanics

Minimal playable core loop: carve the sea, cut off regions without enemies, capture 75% of the field. Built code-first (single main.gd, no assets) via Claude Code + godot-mcp. Tron palette matching the concept art.

Rules

  • On captured land you are safe and may stand still. In the sea you can't stop and you leave a trail.
  • Return to land → your trail plus every sea region without a ball inside becomes captured territory.
  • A ball touching your trail (or you, mid-sea) costs a life. Crossing your own trail is fatal. 3 lives.

Controls

WASD / Arrowsmove
Rrestart after win / game over
Escquit

How to run

Easiest: double-click play.bat. Or open the project from the Godot Project Manager (it's pinned as a favorite) and press F5 — or ask Mr. Mak to launch it via MCP (run_project).
